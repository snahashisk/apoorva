HAPPY BIRTHDAY APOORVA

The enthusiasm and anticipation that comes around on your best friend’s birthday is something you can’t beat. Most people get engrossed in throwing surprise birthday parties and buying unique gifts, but they end up forgetting the importance of birthday wishes. As a best friend, the least you could do is come up with a well-composed heartfelt message that will speak volumes when your gift won’t. Below is a superior list of birthday wishes that could put you on the list of the world’s best friends. With this combination of short and long wishes, you should have more than enough options.

thank you

I would rather walk with you in the dark, than alone in the light.

you da best

You’re even more beautiful on the inside than you are on the outside.

for you

What you are is God's gift to you, what you become is your gift to God.

dream big

Dream as if you'll live forever, live as if you'll die today.

Ranjha

A dog is the only thing on earth that loves you more than he loves himself.

i am so happy

A circle is round. it has no end. That’s how long I want to be your friend.
